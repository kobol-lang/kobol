To verify the download:

  1. Download the asset from the release tag matching this package version, e.g.
     https://github.com/kobol-lang/kobol/releases/download/v0.1.0/kobol-windows-x86_64.zip

  2. Compute its SHA-256 checksum:
        Get-FileHash kobol-windows-x86_64.zip -Algorithm SHA256
